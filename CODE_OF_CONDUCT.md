# Code of conduct

Be respectful, specific, and constructive. Harassment, discrimination, threats, and
deliberate disruption are not acceptable in project spaces.

When reviewing work, address the behavior of the code and the evidence supporting it,
not the person who submitted it. Assume good intent while still asking for reproducible
checks and clear safety boundaries.

Report conduct concerns privately to `contact@redberet.dev`. Maintainers may edit or
remove comments, reject contributions, or restrict participation when necessary to keep
the project safe and productive.
